Welcome to the Team!

Your guide to a successful start.

We are thrilled to have you join us! This guide is designed to help you navigate your first few weeks at the company. Our goal is to make your transition as smooth as possible.

Your First Day

On your first day, you'll meet your team, get your equipment set up, and have an orientation session with HR. Please bring two forms of ID for your I-9 verification.

Schedule for Day 1:

  • 9:00 AM: Welcome & HR Orientation
  • 10:30 AM: IT Equipment Setup
  • 12:00 PM: Lunch with your team
  • 1:30 PM: Introduction to your role and projects
  • 4:00 PM: First day check-in with your manager

Company Culture

Our culture is built on collaboration, innovation, and respect. We encourage open communication and value diverse perspectives. Don't hesitate to ask questions and share your ideas.

Essential Tools

You will be given access to the following tools. Please take some time this week to familiarize yourself with them:

  1. Slack: For internal communication.
  2. Jira: For project and task management.
  3. Confluence: Our internal knowledge base.
  4. Google Workspace: For email, calendar, and documents.
