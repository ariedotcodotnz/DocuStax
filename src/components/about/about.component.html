About DocuStax

Your Static Document Library, Simplified

DocuStax is a modern, fast, and SEO-friendly solution for hosting a public-facing library of documents. Built with a static-first approach, it offers unparalleled performance and security without the need for a complex backend.

Full-Text Search
Instantly search across the entire content of your document library. Our powerful client-side search provides immediate results without waiting for a server.
Blazing Fast Performance
As a fully static site, DocuStax serves pre-built files from a CDN, ensuring the fastest possible load times for your users, anywhere in the world.
SEO Optimized
With semantic HTML, structured data, and per-page meta tags, every document is perfectly optimized for search engines to crawl and index.
Responsive Design
A beautiful, modern interface that looks and works great on any device, from mobile phones to desktops.
